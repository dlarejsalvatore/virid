Journeys receives multiple awards in Horizon Interactive Awards with Virid

Written by Emily Short | May 6, 2021 1:00:00 PM

Journeys is a shoe retailer with roots in surf and skate culture that has hundreds of stores across the US and retail websites for both the US and Canada.

Journeys received Silver in Responsive Mobile Design and Gold in eCommerce Shopping Experience.

 

"In its 19th year, the Horizon Interactive Awards was created to recognize excellence in interactive media production worldwide. Each year, those entries are narrowed down to the 'best of the best' to be recognized and 

promoted on an international stage for their excellence," explains the brand.

 

As a longtime client, Virid continues to implement new projects and improvements to Journeys’ site. In 2020, Virid implemented BOPIS services in July for Journeys as well as continued site experience improvement.

 

With extensive site test loading prior to Black Friday weekend, Virid ensured that Journey’s site performed throughout the holiday weekend, while retailer sites with similar traffic crashed.


Last year, Journeys saw a +29% increase in desktop sales and +55% increase in mobile sales year over year.

 

"The Horizon Interactive Awards, a leading international interactive media awards competition, has announced the 2020 award winners to highlight this year’s “best of the best” in interactive media production.

An international panel of judges, consisting of industry professionals with diverse backgrounds evaluated over 50 categories spanning multiple media types including: websites, mobile applications, print media, interactive displays, public exhibits, online advertising, video, email and more. The 2020 winning entries showcase the industry’s best interactive media solutions from some of the top designers, producers and developers all over the globe," states the Horizon Interactive Awards.
