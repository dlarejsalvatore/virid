Current Boutique receives award in Horizon Interactive Awards

Current Boutique is a high-end consignment retailer with stores located in Virginia and Washington D.C. with a newly optimized online shopping experience. 

Current Boutique received Gold Placement in the eCommerce Shopping Experience category in the 2020 Horizon Interactive Awards. 

 

"In its 19th year, the Horizon Interactive Awards was created to recognize excellence in interactive media production worldwide. Each year, those entries are narrowed down to the 'best of the best' to be recognized and promoted on an international stage for their excellence," explains the brand.

 

The brand re-platformed to Shopify in June and Virid created custom integrations for increased customer experience including a custom CRM, and custom integrations for store credit, affiliate links, consignor experience. 

Current Boutique saw a +285% increase in sales year-over-year after integrations from Virid’s development team. 

"The Horizon Interactive Awards, a leading international interactive media awards competition, has announced the 2020 award winners to highlight this year’s “best of the best” in interactive media production.

An international panel of judges, consisting of industry professionals with diverse backgrounds evaluated over 50 categories spanning multiple media types including: websites, mobile applications, print media, interactive displays, public exhibits, online advertising, video, email and more. The 2020 winning entries showcase the industry’s best interactive media solutions from some of the top designers, producers and developers all over the globe," states the Horizon Interactive Awards. 

 

"Working with Virid has opened up a lot of opportunities for us to increase efficiencies in our eCommerce business. And having a team that can make any integration happen to meet our needs has been invaluable.”

                - Carmen Lopez, Current Boutique CEO
