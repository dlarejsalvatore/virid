Virid Websites Win Awards for Mobile Shopping

Written by Emily Short | May 14, 2021 3:47:00 PM

Four of Virid’s clients were recognized as award-winning e-commerce sites in the 2021 Hermes Creative Awards in the Mobile Buying Experience category.  

 
  • Journeys and National Wildlife Federation were given honorable mention. 
  • Little Burgundy was awarded Gold, the second-highest award for their mobile experience.
  • Current Boutique received Platinum for its mobile-shopping experience, the highest honors given by HCA. 

“Hermes Creative Awards honors the messengers and creators of the information revolution. Armed with their imaginations and computers, Hermes winners bring their ideas to life through traditional and digital platforms. Each year, competition judges evaluate the creative industry’s best publications, branding collateral, websites, videos, and advertising, marketing, and communication programs.” 


There are many aspects that contribute to award-winning mobile website design. These include a responsive design that adapts to a variety of sizes and devices. Shopping sites should also easily allow for a checkout and cart experience that prioritizes mobile needs. It is an important piece of e-commerce websites for businesses. At Virid, we have seen that mobile-first improvements can easily pay off by improving user experience and ease of shopping. 

 

“Hermes Creative Awards is administered by the Association of Marketing and Communication Professionals (AMCP). The international organization consists of several thousand marketing, communication, advertising, public relations, digital media production, and freelance professionals. AMCP oversees awards and recognition programs, provides judges, and awards outstanding achievement and service to the profession,” explains HCA.
